<p class="third centered thirdHeader bigText">The Palace of fun</p>
<p class="centered second bigText">
The palace of fun is an arcade built for people of all ages and personalities, no matter who you are you can’t say no to old and fun arcade games with loved ones. The palace of fun was truly a place that sparked the child in me, and its quite cheap fun as well! </p>

<p class="third centered thirdHeader bigText"> Rides and attractions </p>
<p class="centered second bigText"> This is for people who want excitement and fun, this is for all ages as well! This place is jam packed with fun. Brighton pier have a lot of attractions like bouncy castles, twister, horror hotel and so much more! The one that ended up hogging my time was the wild river! I recommend that ride over all the others!</p>
</section>

 <p class="centered second bigText"> 
If these reasons were not enough to draw you in to this place, I hope these reasons underneath will. At its core the Brighton Pier is a place for fun and relaxation, it fits well with its location by the sea. The innocent fun at the Pier is something I want everyone to explore! There is no excuse to not visit this place if you ever find yourself in Brighton! If my words haven’t convinced you, I hope the <a target="_blank" href="http://www.brightonpier.co.uk/">Brighton Pier home page</a> will.
